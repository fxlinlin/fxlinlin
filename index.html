Short Bio

I graduated from Tianjin University, China with Bachelor and Master degree in 2004 and 2007, respectively, and obtained my Ph.D degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2012. Now I am an assistant professor in the College of Electronics and Information Engineering, Tongji University, China.

Research Interest

Molecular communication, smart wearable devices.
